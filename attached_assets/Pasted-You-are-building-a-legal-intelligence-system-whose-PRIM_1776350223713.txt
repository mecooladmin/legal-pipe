You are building a legal intelligence system whose PRIMARY objective is:

Reconstruct a complete, accurate, and chronological narrative of a legal case from 743 mixed-format documents (Arabic + English), with full citations.

---

## CORE OUTPUT REQUIREMENT

Generate:

1. A fully ordered timeline of events (date-sorted)
2. Merged events across multiple documents
3. Contradictions between sources
4. A final **well-written legal narrative document** that:

   * is chronological
   * is structured
   * includes citations (file + page)
   * distinguishes facts vs claims

---

## CRITICAL NEW LAYER: EVENT RECONSTRUCTION ENGINE

For EACH text chunk:

Extract structured events:

```json
{
  "event_id": "",
  "date_raw": "",
  "date_normalized": "YYYY-MM-DD",
  "event_type": "meeting|contract|payment|incident|claim",
  "description": "",
  "actors": [],
  "location": "",
  "source_file": "",
  "page": "",
  "language": "ar|en",
  "confidence": 0.0
}
```

---

## DATE NORMALIZATION (MANDATORY)

* Handle:

  * Arabic dates (e.g. ١٢ مارس ٢٠٢١)
  * English formats
* Convert ALL to ISO format

---

## EVENT MERGING LOGIC

* Group events by:

  * same date OR close dates
  * similar semantic meaning
* Merge into unified events
* Preserve ALL citations

---

## CONTRADICTION DETECTION

* Detect:

  * conflicting dates
  * conflicting claims
* Flag clearly in output

---

## FINAL TIMELINE GENERATION

* Sort all merged events by date
* Output:

  * timeline.json
  * timeline.md (human-readable)

---

## FINAL LEGAL NARRATIVE

Generate a structured document:

Sections:

* Case Overview
* Chronological Events
* Key Turning Points
* Conflicting Evidence
* Conclusion

Each statement MUST include citations.

---

## PRIORITY ORDER

1. Accuracy of timeline
2. Correct date normalization
3. Proper merging of events
4. Clear narrative generation
5. Performance optimization

---

## SUCCESS CRITERIA

The system is successful ONLY if:

* It reconstructs the case chronologically
* It merges duplicate events across files
* It handles Arabic + English correctly
* It produces a readable legal document with citations
